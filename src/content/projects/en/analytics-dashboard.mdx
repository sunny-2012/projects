The Analytics Dashboard is a powerful business intelligence platform designed to transform raw sales data into actionable insights. Built for enterprise teams, it consolidates key metrics across revenue, customer acquisition, and product performance into a single, unified interface.

The dashboard was developed with a focus on real-time data streaming and interactive visualizations, enabling sales managers and executives to monitor KPIs, track conversion funnels, and identify trends — all without any SQL or data science expertise required.

Key features:

- Real-time revenue tracking with daily, weekly, and monthly breakdowns
- Cohort analysis and customer retention heatmaps
- Funnel visualization for lead-to-close conversion tracking
- Customizable KPI widgets with drag-and-drop layout
- Automated alerts for anomalies and goal completions
- Role-based access control and white-label export options
